# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Offering and accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing criticism on ideas and implementations, not people

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation
- Publishing others private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers (here: The SNEK Initiative) are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that violate this Code of Conduct.
